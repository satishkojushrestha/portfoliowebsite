Impact of Technology

Technology is being used prudently these days. Most of the people used it for different purpose such as entertainment , chatting, learning and so on. As we knew that society has been dramatically changed with the evolution of technology. Various opportunities are being provided by technologies as it play vital role in human life. Directly or indirectly it effects our society , health , education ,etc similarly, showcasing its advantage and disadvantage .

With the help of technology , used of car is possible which depend on fossil fuel. From the recent data , we can observed that almost all the people adopted this technology and we know fossil fuel is non renewable source of energy . If we practice same technology then one day we need to face sacristy of energy. Likewise new and advanced technology encourage people to established various industries based on need . As they work so hard to present the best technology for the both society and business, the industry used harmful chemical and released the waste product to the environment which leads to global warming. So the more technology we enjoy, the more we damage our environment. Scientist are trying to reduce the impact by inventing alternative gadgets which runs from renewable source of energy or you can say green cars ,green computing , and so on.

However the technology brought drastic change in peoples life. We heard from our parents that they have to work all day in field without any instrument. With the help of advanced technology , different types of instrument are made which makes their work easier. They tell us that technology is boon for them. Manufacturing of genetic crops is possible , and also helps to grow fast. The artificial fertilizers is also available in market. Many unemployed farmer are employed. Employment percent is increasing day by day. Another important field is transportation. Transportation is one of the basic area where the experts have to used their ideas as well as , it provides mobility for the people and good. Similarly for the development of countries and citizens, the transportation is very essential.

To sum up it may be concluded from the forgoing points that technology have positive as well as negative impact . The fact is , it is important for people to learn and understand technology.
